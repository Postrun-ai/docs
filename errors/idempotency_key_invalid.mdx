The Idempotency-Key header is malformed.

## When it happens

An `Idempotency-Key` header was present but empty or longer than 255 characters. Raised by the idempotency middleware before the handler runs.

## How to resolve

Send a non-empty key of at most 255 characters — a UUID or other unique, opaque string. This is a client bug; fix the header and resend.
