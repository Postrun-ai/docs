The requested resource does not exist for this account.

## When it happens

A single-resource fetch/mutation references an id that is absent, or that belongs to another account. Existence is never leaked across accounts, so a foreign resource also returns 404 (not 403).

## How to resolve

Verify the id and that it belongs to the authenticated account. If you keyed it from your own `external_id`, re-resolve the mapping. Show your user: "We could not find that item."

## Reference
