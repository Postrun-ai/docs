The image aspect ratio is outside the platform’s accepted band.

## When it happens

On `POST /media`, an image’s width:height ratio falls outside a target platform’s accepted range (Postrun never crops or guesses framing). Also surfaced at publish for an Instagram FEED single image outside 4:5–1.91:1.

## How to resolve

Crop or pad the image into the accepted band (`allowed` names it) and re-upload, or, for Instagram, post it as a carousel item (which crops to fit). Show your user: "This image’s shape isn’t allowed on &lt;platform>."

## Reference

| --- | --- |
| Code | `media_aspect_ratio_unsupported` |

| Retryable | No — the request must change before it can succeed. |
