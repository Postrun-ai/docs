The Instagram media container failed processing.

## When it happens

The container reached `status_code: ERROR` — Instagram could not process the media. Returned as the Instagram variant’s `outcome.error`.

## How to resolve

Re-check the media against Instagram’s requirements, re-upload if needed, and republish. The forwarded detail gives the reason where Instagram provides one. Show your user: "Instagram couldn’t process this media."
