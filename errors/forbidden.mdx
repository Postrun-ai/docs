The credential is valid but not permitted to do this.

## When it happens

A read-only secret key attempted a write, OR a frontend token lacks the required scope or is bound to a profile it may not touch. Enforced globally by the scope/binding gate in `base.ts`.

## How to resolve

Use a write-enabled key for mutating calls, or mint a token whose scopes include the action and whose `profiles` claim covers the target. Money-value ad writes require the secret-key-only `ads:budget` scope — never mintable into a browser token.
