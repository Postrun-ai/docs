The variant declares mutually-exclusive content shapes.

## When it happens

A publish variant combines shapes that cannot coexist — e.g. an X post with more than one of media/poll/quote/card; a LinkedIn post with both article and poll, or a content sub-object that contradicts its `content_kind`; a Facebook link on a non-text post. Surfaced in the `not_publishable` `errors[]`.

## How to resolve

Keep only one content shape per the platform’s rules and remove the conflicting field(s), then resubmit. The error path points at the offending field.
