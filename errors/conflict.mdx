The request conflicts with the resource’s current state.

## When it happens

A write contradicts the resource as it stands now — e.g. a uniqueness collision (`external_id` already in use) or a state transition that is not legal from the current state. The generic 409; specific conflicts carry their own code.

## How to resolve

Re-fetch the resource, reconcile the difference, and retry with a non-conflicting request. For an `external_id` collision, choose a unique value.
