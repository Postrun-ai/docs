One or more variants are not ready to publish.

## When it happens

A publish call (`now` / `schedule` / `dry_run`) ran the readiness gate and at least one variant failed. The 422 body’s `errors[]` carries the per-variant `not_publishable` domain codes (content/media/placement reasons) with their `variant_index` and `path`.

## How to resolve

Inspect each item in `errors[]`, fix the named variant problems (see the specific domain codes — `content_missing`, `media_not_ready`, etc.), and resubmit. Some inner reasons (e.g. `media_not_ready`) become resolvable once processing completes.

## Reference

| Retryable | No — the request must change before it can succeed. |
