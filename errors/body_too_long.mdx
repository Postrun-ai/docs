The post body exceeds the platform’s character limit.

## When it happens

A publish variant’s body is over the platform cap — X weighted length (280, or 25,000 for Premium long-form), LinkedIn 3,000, Instagram 2,200, Facebook 63,206. X counts weighted length (URLs = 23 chars, CJK/emoji weighted). Surfaced in the `not_publishable` `errors[]`.

## How to resolve

Shorten the body under the limit (`allowed`/`got` give the cap and the measured length) and resubmit. For X, remember URLs and emoji are weighted. Show your user a live character counter.
