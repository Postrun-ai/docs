The connection must be reconnected before selecting an account.

## When it happens

On account-selection, the short-lived platform token (e.g. a Facebook user token) had already expired before the account could be selected.

## How to resolve

Send the user back through the OAuth connect flow to refresh the grant, then retry account selection. Show your user: "Please reconnect your account to continue."

## Reference

| Retryable | No — the request must change before it can succeed. |
