The Instagram media container expired before publishing.

## When it happens

The container reached `status_code: EXPIRED` — it was not published within Instagram’s 24-hour window. Terminal. Returned as the Instagram variant’s `outcome.error`.

## How to resolve

Re-create the container by republishing from scratch (it cannot be reused). This is not a wait-and-retry case; the old container is dead.
