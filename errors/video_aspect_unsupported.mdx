The video aspect ratio is outside the platform’s accepted band.

## When it happens

On `POST /media`, the video’s width:height ratio falls outside the target platform’s documented aspect range. Returned per-platform with the accepted bounds in `allowed`.

## How to resolve

Re-frame or pad the video into the accepted aspect band and re-upload, or drop the platform. Show your user: "This video’s shape isn’t allowed on &lt;platform>."

## Reference

| Field | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Code | `video_aspect_unsupported` |
| Channel | Domain verdict — returned inside a 200 response |
| Retryable | No — the request must change before it can succeed. |
